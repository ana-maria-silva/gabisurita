About

Who I am

I'm a Senior Research Engineer at Google DeepMind, where I work on making AI agents better at writing code. I care deeply about using AI to empower programmers — not replace them.

I graduated from Unicamp (University of Campinas) in 2018. Beyond code generation, my research interests include reinforcement learning (RL) methods and combinatorial optimization.

Outside of my core work at DeepMind, I enjoy teaching programming and AI, demystifying the field to others.

Outside of working hours, you can find me in nature or doing sports. I also enjoy critical theory and philosophy. There's so much more to life than just computers.
